How to Use This Guide

Scroll through the photo sections below. Match your port to one of the examples. We include close-ups, thread views, and adapter compatibility to help you be sure.

Beckson 6" Screw-In

The most common port on small sailboats. Three-lug design, coarse threads, usually white plastic.

Adapter Needed: None – fits directly.

Beckson 4" Screw-In

Same brand, smaller diameter. Threads and lugs similar to the 6" version, but tighter radius.

Adapter Needed: Hull Dryer Mini fits directly.

RWO 6" Screw-In

Distinctive fine threads and black plastic ring. Common on older UK-built boats.

Adapter Needed: RWO 6" adapter ring

Sea-Dog 6" Screw-In

Coarse, wide threads. Lighter plastic than Beckson. Often found on budget-friendly boats.

Adapter Needed: Sea-Dog 6" screw-in adapter

Sea-Dog 6" Quarter-Turn

Locks with a quarter twist instead of threads. Has locking tabs and no visible threads.

Adapter Needed: Sea-Dog quarter-turn adapter

Seaflo 6" Screw-In

Smooth threads with wider spacing. Usually marked with brand name on the lid.

Adapter Needed: Seaflo 6" adapter ring
