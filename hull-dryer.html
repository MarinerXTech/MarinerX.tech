How It Works

1. Threads directly into an inspection port
Standard offering fits Beckson brand 6" screw-in inspection port with no adapter needed. If you have a different brand or style port, use an optional adapter to fit your boat.

2. Just plug it in
Powered via a 12V DC barrel jack connection (wall adapter included). No switches, apps, or setup required.

3. Circulates air and removes trapped moisture
Actively drying enclosed spaces.

Air enters at the side, is driven downward by the fan, and exits through the hull opening via the inspection port.
