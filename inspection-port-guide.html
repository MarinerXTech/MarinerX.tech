Inspection Port Identification Guide

Identify your inspection port / deck plate quickly using visual diagnostics that are easy to compare.

Identify Your Inspection Port / Deck Plate

Step 1

Size and engagement type

Measure the clear opening and confirm screw-in vs quarter-turn vs pop-out.

Step 2

Brand diagnostics

Look for names and logos in the usual locations.

Step 3

Confirm the details

Confirm by looking at the pictures and detail notes in the sections below.

What to Measure

Measure the clear opening or inside thread area to determine the size family.

Quick checks

  • Measure the clear opening or inside thread area first, not the outside rim.
  • Use cutout and flange dimensions only as secondary confirmation.
  • If dimensions conflict, trust opening size plus thread/seal diagnostics.

Beckson & West Marine / 4-Inch Screw-In

4-inch Beckson / West Marine family; Beckson and West Marine use the same thread profile, and the Hull Dryer fits these directly.

Diagnostic Checks

  • Name/logo: West Marine logo is usually on top of the plate, and Beckson name is usually on the underside of the plate.
  • Thread profile: Asymmetric thread profile.
  • Seal location: O-ring seal on the plate.
  • Measurements: 4-inch nominal opening; about 4-1/2-inch cutout.

Adapter note (if using Hull Dryer): No adapter needed.

Beckson & West Marine / 6-Inch Screw-In

Diagnostic Checks

RWO & Allen / 4-Inch Screw-In

4-inch version of the RWO / Allen metric-pattern family.

Diagnostic Checks

  • Name/logo: RWO logo is typically in the finger depression on top of the plate.
  • Thread profile: Segmented threads in the installed ring (metric-family screw-in pattern).
  • Seal location: O-ring seal on the plate.
  • Measurements: 4-inch nominal class in the RWO / Allen metric-pattern family.

Adapter note (if using Hull Dryer): Use 4-inch RWO or 4-inch Allen adapter.

Sea-Dog Line / 6-Inch Screw-In

Sea-Dog screw-in family with logo typically on underside of lid and seal on ring.

Diagnostic Checks

  • Name/logo: Logo is typically on the underside of the top plate.
  • Thread profile: Full screw-in engagement (not quarter-turn tabs).
  • Seal location: Seal is on the ring.
  • Measurements: 6-inch Sea-Dog Line screw-in family.

Adapter note (if using Hull Dryer): Use 6-inch Sea-Dog adapter.

Sea-Dog Line / 6-Inch Quarter-Turn

Sea-Dog quarter-turn version uses cam-lock engagement instead of a full screw thread, so it falls outside the supported Hull Dryer fit range.

Diagnostic Checks

  • Name/logo: Sea-Dog branding is typically molded into the underside of the lid.
  • Lock profile: Quarter-turn cam tabs engage with short slots instead of a continuous screw thread.
  • Seal location: Seal is on the ring.
  • Measurements: 6-inch Sea-Dog Line quarter-turn family.

Adapter note (if using Hull Dryer): Not currently supported.
