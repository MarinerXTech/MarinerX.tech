What Is the Hull Dryer?

When water gets inside the hull, it can really weigh you down and risks damage to your boat. Drying out the inside of the hull usually meant cobbling something together. We designed a clean, purpose-built solution—and tested it to optimize airflow and drying performance. The MarinerX Hull Dryer fits securely, works efficiently, and simplifies the whole process.

The MarinerX Hull Dryer actively removes moisture trapped inside your boat’s hull using the inspection port as an interface. It's ideal for small craft such as Sunfish and Laser sailboats and any other boat with similar access points.

Standard fits Beckson 6" screw-in inspection ports, with adapter rings for RWO, Allen, Sea-Dog, Seaflo, and more to come.

How It Works

1. Threads directly into an inspection port
Standard offering fits Beckson brand 6" screw-in inspection port with no adapter needed. If you have a different brand or style port, use an optional adapter to fit your boat.

2. Just plug it in
Powered via a 12V DC barrel jack connection (wall adapter included). No switches, apps, or setup required.

3. Circulates air and removes trapped moisture
Actively drying enclosed spaces.

Specs

  • Fits Beckson 6" threaded inspection ports (standard version) - optional adapter rings available
  • 12V DC power input through barrel connector (wall adapter with barrel plug included)
  • IP68 waterproof-rated fan
  • Draws 11 Watts
  • Delivers 101 CFM airflow

Looking for compatibility with another port type? Let us know — we can design and 3D print alternate interfaces!

⚠️ Safety Notice

The MarinerX Hull Dryer is not intended for use in engine compartments or spaces containing fuel, fuel vapors, or other flammable substances.

It is designed for hull compartments where water intrusion may occur, but should not be submerged or exposed to direct spray or standing water.

Operates on 12VDC (AC adapter included). When using shore power, connect to a GFCI-protected outlet when practical.
