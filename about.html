About MarinerX

Practical sailing tools, engineered from real-world problems.

Our Mission

MarinerX was founded with a vision to develop innovative and practical solutions for boat owners. Our goal is to enhance the sailing experience by tackling common problems with effective, well-engineered products.

Our Story

MarinerX started as a passion project, combining a love for sailing and hands-on engineering. Our first product, the MarinerX Hull Dryer, was designed to address the issue of trapped moisture in boat hulls, helping owners prevent damage and maintain peak performance.

That same approach continues to guide new designs: identify a real problem, prototype solutions, test them in the real world, and refine until the result is simple, reliable, and genuinely useful.

Our Philosophy

MarinerX products are designed to be simple, durable, and genuinely useful. Each design should solve a real boating problem without unnecessary complexity. Founded by Drew, an engineer who enjoys developing practical solutions to interesting problems. That same engineering mindset guides every MarinerX product: understand the problem clearly, prototype thoughtfully, and refine until the solution works the way it should.
