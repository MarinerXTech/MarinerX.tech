A secure place for your rig to ride.

Tired of balancing spars, sails, and rigging across your deck or trailer? RigNest gives your gear a place to go.

Designed to anchor into your mast step, RigNest creates a clean, secure tie-down point for your Sunfish or other small dinghy rig. Built from glass fiber-reinforced ASA, it's rugged enough to handle road vibration, UV exposure, and wet conditions—season after season.

Pair it with optional add-ons to store your daggerboard, rudder, or tiller, and you’ve got an all-in-one transport and storage solution for your entire rig.

Key Benefits

  • Mounts directly into your mast step—no drilling required
  • Keeps your rig stable and upright for trailering or storage
  • Made from marine-grade glass fiber-reinforced ASA
  • Designed for modular accessories (coming soon)
  • Simple to install. Secure. Purpose-built.
  • Designed for Sunfish and other small sailboats with 2" diameter mast.

RigNest isn’t just a product. It’s peace of mind on the road—and one less thing to wrestle with on shore.
